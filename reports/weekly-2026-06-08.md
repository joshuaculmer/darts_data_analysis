- **Q1.1 ‚ÄĒ AI condition ‚Üí scorePerHit.** Status ūüü° (insufficient n). Finding: Kruskal‚ÄďWallis
  H=3.29, df=6. Descriptive direction: NONE (6.75) is ~1.2 pts/hit lower than AI-advice
  conditions (range 7.52‚Äď9.23), consistent with AI advice helping performance. However, NONE
  sessions have higher mean skill (60 vs 29‚Äď40 for other conditions), which likely suppresses
  the NONE advantage. With n‚Č§6 per cell and no control for skill, no inference is supportable.
  Confidence: Very low (1 complete participant, skill imbalance across conditions).

- **Q1.2 ‚ÄĒ Trajectory 5 vs 6 (HEADLINE).** Status ūüü° (n too small). Finding: GOOD_PLAUSIBLE
  (condition 5) has only 1 session (scorePerHit=9.23); PLAUSIBLE_GOOD (condition 6) has 3

  Confidence: Uninterpretable ‚ÄĒ at least 2 sessions per arm are required for effect-size
  estimation; ~10+ per arm for meaningful power.

- **Q1.3 ‚ÄĒ AI ‚Üí proxOptimal.** Status ūüü° (small n). Finding: CORRECT has the lowest proxOptimal

  may still reduce proxOptimal vs no advice. Confidence: Low (n‚Č§6 per cell, skill confound).

  r=+0.58 (n=24). Higher scorePerHit sessions are associated with higher satisfaction.

  Confidence: Low (n=24 sessions, 2 participants; collapses person structure).
